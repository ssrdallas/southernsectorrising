## Key Observations (10 min)

### What's Working Well
1. **Strong founding narrative** - Shingle Mountain victory is compelling
2. **Dr. Jackson's credentials** - Impressive recognition and media coverage
3. **Clean current design** - Professional Squarespace foundation
4. **Clear mission** - Environmental justice message comes through

### Critical Gaps I Identified

| Gap | Impact on Funding/Programs |
|-----|---------------------------|
| **Scope not reflected** | Funders see local org, not regional/national/international player |
| **150+ allies invisible** | Coalition strength hidden; looks like solo operation |
| **No current work featured** | Kenya, Rome delegations missing; appears inactive |
| **No impact metrics** | Hard for funders to quantify investment value |
| **Focus areas scattered** | Five pillars not clearly organized |

## Discovery Questions

Before showing my recommendations, I'd like to understand:

### About Your International Work
1. What was your role in the Kenya delegation? Rome?
2. Are there photos/stories we can feature?
3. Which international allies should we highlight?

### About Current Programs
1. Beyond Sandbranch and the Park for Floral Farms, what else is active?
2. What's the status of the Community HUBs initiative?
3. Any upcoming policy wins or campaigns?

### About Your Allies
1. Can we get logos/names of key partners for the website?
2. Are there testimonial quotes we can use?
3. How should we categorize the 150+ organizations?

### About Funding Goals
1. Who are your target funders? (foundations, government, individual donors)
2. What's your annual giving campaign strategy?
3. Are there specific grants you're pursuing that need supporting materials?

## Implementation Discussion (10 min)

### Option A: Refresh Current Site
- Update content within existing Squarespace
- Add new sections matching MVP structure
- Lower investment, faster execution

### Option B: Full Redesign
- New design system and branding
- Custom development or premium template
- Maximum impact for major funding push

### Option C: Phased Approach
- Phase 1: Critical content updates (international work, allies, metrics)
- Phase 2: Visual refresh and new sections
- Phase 3: Full platform evaluation if needed
