2. Project Scope — Starter Package

This agreement covers the design and development of a professional website for Southern Sector Rising under the Starter Package. Given the breadth of SSR's programs, international presence, and ally network, the project scope reflects 8–10 core pages to adequately represent the organization.

Deliverable Details Type Value
Website Design & Development 8–10 core pages: Home, About, Programs/Our Work, Impact, Global Reach, Allies & Partners, Media, Events, Get Involved, Contact Essential $2,400
Mobile-Responsive Build Fully optimized for mobile, tablet, and desktop devices Essential $500
Donation Integration Stripe or PayPal embedded giving form with suggested amounts Essential $400
Contact Form Setup Contact form routed to SSR's preferred inbox Essential $200
Basic SEO Setup Page titles, meta descriptions, XML sitemap, image optimization Included $400
Post-Launch Revision Round 1 consolidated round of feedback applied after launch Included $300
Post-Launch Support Window 2 weeks of bug fixes and minor adjustments Included $200
Total Itemized Value $4,400
Your Starter Package Rate $4,000

The packaged rate reflects a $400 discount off itemized pricing as a foundation for a long-term partnership.

Out of Scope (available in Growth package upgrade): Custom animations, coalition partner logo carousel, interactive events calendar, CMS for self-editing, accessibility compliance audit, multilingual support, grant-ready impact reporting pages, annual report design.

5. Terms & Conditions

Revisions: This agreement includes 1 consolidated round of revisions post-launch. Additional revisions beyond this scope will be quoted separately at an agreed hourly rate.
Ownership & IP: Full ownership of the completed website transfers to Southern Sector Rising upon receipt of final payment. The Service Provider retains the right to display the work in their portfolio and marketing materials.
Content Supply: The Client is responsible for providing all written content, logos, photographs, and brand materials in a timely manner. Delays in content delivery may affect the project timeline. If content is not provided within 30 days of a milestone, the Service Provider may proceed with placeholder content.
Hosting & Domain: Hosting and domain registration are not included in this agreement and remain the Client's responsibility unless otherwise agreed in writing.
Maintenance: Ongoing maintenance and updates beyond the 2-week post-launch support window are not included. A separate maintenance retainer will be proposed after launch for the Client's consideration.
Cancellation: If the Client cancels after work has begun, the deposit is non-refundable. All work completed to date will be delivered to the Client. If the Client cancels before work begins, the deposit will be refunded in full.
Confidentiality: Both parties agree to keep confidential any sensitive information shared during the course of this project.
Governing Law: This agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of Texas. Any disputes shall first be attempted to be resolved through good-faith negotiation.
