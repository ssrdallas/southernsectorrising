# Southern Sector Rising Website Redesign
## Strategic Analysis & Proposal

---

## Executive Summary

Southern Sector Rising (SSR) has evolved from a local grassroots movement fighting the Shingle Mountain crisis into a **regional, national, and international force** with 150+ allies working on environmental justice, climate resilience, food sovereignty, and labor rights. However, the current website does not reflect this growth or position SSR for the funding and partnership opportunities this expanded scope creates.

---

## Current Website Assessment

### Strengths
- Clean, professional Squarespace foundation
- Strong founding narrative (Shingle Mountain victory)
- Dr. Marsha Jackson's credentials and recognition well-documented
- Mobile responsive design
- Clear mission articulation

### Critical Gaps

| Area | Current State | Needed State |
|------|---------------|--------------|
| **Scope** | Appears primarily local/Dallas-focused | Showcase Regional → National → International reach |
| **Allies/Partners** | No visibility | Feature 150+ ally network prominently |
| **Current Work** | Mostly historical (Shingle Mountain) | Highlight Kenya, Rome delegations, current projects |
| **Impact Metrics** | None visible | Quantifiable achievements, communities served |
| **Focus Areas** | Mentioned but not organized | Dedicated sections for each pillar |
| **Funding Appeal** | Basic donate button | Compelling case for institutional funders |
| **Visual Identity** | Generic nonprofit look | Distinctive, movement-aligned branding |

---

## Recommended Structure

### Primary Navigation
```
Home | About | Our Work | Impact | Allies & Partners | Media | Get Involved | Donate
```

### New/Expanded Sections

1. **Our Work (Pillar-Based)**
   - Environmental Justice
   - Climate Resilience
   - Food Sovereignty
   - Labor & Economic Justice
   - Community HUBs

2. **Geographic Reach**
   - Local (Dallas/Southern Sector)
   - Regional (Texas/Southwest)
   - National (US partnerships)
   - International (Kenya, Rome, global allies)

3. **Impact Dashboard**
   - Communities served
   - Allies in network
   - Policy wins
   - Water deliveries
   - People reached through tours

4. **International Work** (NEW)
   - Delegate experiences
   - Global partnerships
   - Climate justice on world stage

5. **Ally Network**
   - Partner logos
   - Coalition memberships
   - Testimonials from partners

## Visual Rebrand Recommendations

### Color Palette
| Color | Hex | Usage |
|-------|-----|-------|
| Deep Forest | #1B4332 | Primary headers, trust |
| Earth Gold | #D4A534 | Accents, CTAs, hope |
| Sky Blue | #4A90A4 | Secondary, water/air themes |
| Warm Sand | #F5E6D3 | Backgrounds, warmth |
| Rich Soil | #5C4033 | Text, groundedness |

### Typography
- **Headlines:** Playfair Display (authority, legacy)
- **Body:** Inter (modern, readable)
- **Accents:** Oswald (bold statements)

### Visual Themes
- Root/tree imagery (growth, grounding)
- Rising sun motifs (hope, new day)
- Community gathering imagery
- International photography (Kenya, Rome)
- Maps showing geographic reach
